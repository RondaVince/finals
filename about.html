ABOUT TESDA AUTOMOTIVE

Tesda automotive course is a training in selected automotive shop courses such as engine repair, suspension and steering, automotive electronics.
This course is recognized by DTI (Department of Trade and Industry) and TESDA (Technical Education and Skills Development Authority).
The duration of the class varies depending on your chosen program or course.
Tesda automotive course is composed of four (4) levels of training, which are TESDA NC II, TESDA NC III, TESDA NC IV-A and TESDA NC IV-B.
